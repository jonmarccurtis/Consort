My Notes Filtering

This form tells what voice parts you have been assigned for each song.  These assignments are also used to create your "My notes" filter.  Each note has a list of voice parts it pertains to.  That list is compared with your assignments, and when there is a match, the note is one of "yours", an X is displayed in the "My note" column, and the note shows up when you use one of the "My note" filter presets.

The form also enables you to override the Director's assignments so that you can have more control over what your "My note" filter includes.  Here are a couple reasons why you might want to do this:
  • You've been reassigned to a different voice part, but the Director's assignments hasn't been updated yet.
  • You are singing T2, but you want to monitor the T1 notes and have them show up in your filtered list.

Check box to override
When you click the box, another set of voice parts appears that you can edit.  These settings will not affect your Director assigned voice parts, but will be used by the "My notes" filter.  Be sure to Save your changes when done.
